Read $ARGUMENTS (file path or inline code).

Explain it as if to a competent engineer who's unfamiliar with this specific code:

1. **What it does** — the high-level purpose in one sentence
2. **How it works** — walk through the logic step by step, plain English
3. **Key decisions** — any non-obvious design choices and why they might exist
4. **Inputs / Outputs** — what it expects and what it returns/produces
5. **Edge cases** — what happens on empty input, errors, or unusual data
6. **Dependencies** — what external things it relies on

Avoid jargon unless necessary. If something looks wrong or fragile, say so.
